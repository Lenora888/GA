Located in Central Harlem, New York, International Franks is truly a destination that is worth the trip. When you eat our dogs, you will know that you are eating "the best dog in town". We've put an international twist on a good old American menu, while offering many combinations to create a meal that will satisfy each time! After one visit, you will be sure to be back again and again for one the city's most delicious hot dogs. Bring your appetite and enjoy the hot dog you create using the freshest ingredients.
