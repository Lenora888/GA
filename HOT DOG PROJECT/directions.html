DIRECTIONS PAGE
International Franks is located in Central Harlem just a couple of blocks from 145th Street.
We are conveniently located near the 2 and 3 lines, where you can also find a myriad of buses, including the M100 and M101.
Additionally, you may find the A,B,C,D trains at 145th and St. Nicholas Avenue.
