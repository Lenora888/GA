RULES OF THE GAME......

  • Rock beats scissor, but loses to paper
  • Paper beats rock, but loses to scissors
  • Scissor beats paper, but loses to rock
    • INSTRUCTIONS:
    • The game will not begin until the START BUTTON IS PRESSED and the GREEN LIGHT TURNS ON.
    • The players begin by counting down Three, two, one, go! The players then choose the image of the hand gesture for their selection.

    • To select Rock, choose the image of the hand with the closed fist.
    • To select Scissors, choose the image of the flat hand with the pointer finger and the middle finger extended and separated.
    • To select Paper, choose the image of the flat open hand.
